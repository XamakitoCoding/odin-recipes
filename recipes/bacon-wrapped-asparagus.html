Bacon Wrapped Asparagus

These bacon wrapped asparagus spears are full of flavor!
Perfect for a delicious springtime appetizer or side dish!

Ingredients

  • Fresh asparagus
  • Olive oil
  • Bacon slices
  • Black pepper

Step-by-step

  1. Place asparagus in a large bowl and add olive oil to coat each spear. Wrap them with bacon slices to taste and repeat till all the asaparagus are wrapped.
  2. Preheat an outdoor grill for medium heat and add the spears until the bacon is crispy, season with peper.
  3. Serve!
