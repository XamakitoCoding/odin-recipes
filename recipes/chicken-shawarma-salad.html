Chicken Shawarma Salad

An incredible recipe to these times where everyone is trying to be healthy.
An easy, fresh and high in protein dish to share!

Ingredients

  • Two lemons, olive oil, 3 garlic cloves
  • 2 cracked black pepper, ground cumin and paprika teaspoons, 1 kosher salt teaspoon, and half a teaspoon of ground turmeric and red pepper flakes
  • Boneless chicken!
  • Tahini, olive oil, water, lemon, sea salt and black pepper
  • Fresh parsley, dried basil leaves, salt, italian seasoning, fennel seeds, and black pepper
  • Lettuce, fresh parsley, fresh mint, 2 cups of halved cherry tomatoes, english cucumber and red onion

Step-by-step

  1. Combine the olive oil, lemon juice, garlic, seasonings in a bowl and whisk
  2. Place the chicken into a bag and pour the marinade, seal an place in the refrigerator for at least 3 hours
  3. Preheat the oven to 220 degrees Celsius
  4. Remove chicken from the bag and place it in the oven for about 30 minutes
  5. Place tahini, olive oil, 1/4 cup of water, lemon juice, salt and pepper into a blender, then emulsify and add water to reach desired consistency
  6. Chop lettuce and combine it with parsley and mint in a bowl, layer tomatoes, cucumber and red onion. Then, top with the chicken and the dressing
  7. Serve!
