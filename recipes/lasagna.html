Lasagna

A lasagna is worth the waiting! Just be sure to make it with love and a lot of cheese.
In under 2 hours you should be enjoying with ur friends or family!

Ingredients

  • Italian sausage and ground beef
  • An entire onion and two cloves of garlic
  • Crushed tomatoes, two cans of tomato sauce and two cans of tomato paste
  • Two tablespoons of white sugar
  • Fresh parsley, dried basil leaves, salt, italian seasoning, fennel seeds, and black pepper
  • Lasagna noodles (preferible made by you)
  • Parmesan, mozarrela and ricotta cheese
  • Just one egg

Step-by-step

  1. Gather all the ingredients
  2. Cook the sausage, the ground beef, onion and garlic together until well browned
  3. Stir in crushed tomatoes, tomato sauce, tomato paste and a little bit of water. Season it with sugar, 2 tablespoons of parsley, basil, 1 of salt, italian seasoning, fennel seeds, and pepper. Simmer, covered, for about 1 and a half hour, stirring occasionally
  4. Cook lasagna noodles in boiling water until ready, then drain and rinse
  5. In a mixing bowl, combine ricotta cheese with egg, two tablespoons parsley and half a teaspoon of salt
  6. Preheat the oven to 190 degrees Celsius
  7. Layer the lasagna with all you just prepared
  8. Cover with foil and bake in the oven for 25 minutes, then another 25 minutes without the foil
  9. Serve!
